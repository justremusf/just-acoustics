## Article Brief Template

Each article should include:

- H1 matching the search intent.
- Short answer in the first 100 words.
- Singapore-specific context.
- Practical buying guidance.
- When to use acoustic panels vs other treatment.
- CTA to send room photos or book a consultation.
- Links to one service page, one product page, and one project page.

### Ceiling Panels vs Wall Panels

Intent: Buyer deciding where panels should go and what will look best.  
Angle: Compare aesthetics, placement constraints, reflection control, installation considerations, and acoustic performance.  
Include: when wall panels work best, when ceiling panels are better, how design affects the decision, and how to combine both without over-treating the room.

Status: Priority new gap. Keep the topic about ceiling panels versus wall panels, not ceiling baffles.

### Custom Print / Fabric Finish Panels

Intent: Buyer wants treatment that does not look industrial or temporary.  
Angle: Acoustic panels can be designed into the room rather than added as an afterthought.  
Include: brand colours, artwork/custom print, fabric finish, interior design coordination, offices, restaurants, churches, and studios.

Status: Useful near-term replacement for PET content.

## Content Quality Rules

- Do not publish generic acoustics articles without a conversion path.
- Add local examples and project references wherever possible.
- Use original photos over stock images.
- Keep definitions short and quotable.
- Link every guide to a service or product page.
- Update published content when new projects create stronger proof.
- Do not plan PET-specific articles until PET panels become a stronger sales priority.
- Do not create project case studies without first asking for project-specific details.
