# SEO Strategy: Just Acoustics

Analyzed: 2026-05-02  
Site: https://www.justacoustics.co  
Business type: Singapore local service business for acoustic treatment, acoustic panels, echo control, and installation.

## Recommendation

Prioritize qualified commercial search over broad acoustic education. Just Acoustics should win high-intent Singapore queries where the buyer has a room problem now: office echo, restaurant noise, church speech clarity, tuition-centre noise, home-studio treatment, acoustic panel installation, and acoustic panel pricing.

The core strategy is:

1. Fix crawler trust issues first.
2. Build service and use-case pages around buyer intent.
3. Turn project proof into the main differentiator.
4. Publish practical buying guides that answer cost, panel quantity, RT60, and treatment-vs-soundproofing questions.
5. Strengthen local authority through reviews, project photos, Google Business Profile consistency, and Singapore-specific examples.

## Current Position

Cached audit data from 2026-05-01 scores the site at 65/100 overall. Strengths include indexable server-rendered pages, strong image alt coverage, a useful service/blog base, `llms.txt`, and dedicated pages for offices, restaurants, churches, education, gyms, studios, products, and projects.

The main constraint is not lack of content. It is signal consistency and commercial prioritization.

## Priority Issues

1. Mixed host signals across redirects, sitemap, canonicals, and `llms.txt`.
2. Missing canonicals on core pages.
3. `llms.txt` previously referenced `/pricing` as a core page when the audit saw a 404.
4. `/shop` discoverability should stay aligned across sitemap, internal links, and AI surfaces.
5. Several money-page titles and meta descriptions need tightening.

## Target Audience

- Business owners with noisy restaurants, cafes, bars, offices, schools, tuition centres, gyms, churches, studios, and event spaces.
- Interior designers, contractors, and facility managers needing acoustic panels that look integrated.
- Residential buyers with home studios, HDB echo, condo echo, or video-call problems.
- Buyers comparing acoustic treatment, acoustic panels, sound absorption, and soundproofing.

## Positioning

Just Acoustics should not compete only as a product supplier. The stronger angle is:

> Practical acoustic diagnosis, panel design, 3D proposal, and clean installation for Singapore rooms that need clearer speech and less echo.

This separates the site from catalog-heavy competitors and helps convert buyers who do not know which product they need.

## Conversion Strategy

Every commercial page should have:

- One clear room-specific H1.
- Short explanation of the acoustic problem.
- Recommended treatment types.
- Photos or project examples.
- Expected process: contact, send info, site visit, proposal, installation.
- FAQ section for cost, timeline, disruption, soundproofing difference, and maintenance.
- WhatsApp and quote CTA above the fold and near the end.

## Technical Foundation

- Standardize canonical host on `https://www.justacoustics.co`.
- Keep sitemap, robots, canonical tags, schema URLs, OG URLs, and `llms.txt` on the same host.
- Keep `/pricing` indexable if it exists and is useful; otherwise remove it from crawler guidance.
- Add sitewide canonical metadata to all indexable routes.
- Keep LocalBusiness and Organization schema sitewide, then add Service, Product, Article, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, and Project/CreativeWork style schema where relevant.
- Add baseline security headers and review marketing-page caching.

## AI Search Readiness

Just Acoustics has a good base because the site explains acoustics clearly. Improve AI citation potential with:

- Direct answer blocks on service pages.
- Pricing ranges or cost factors where commercially safe.
- Project-specific before/after explanations.
- Clean `llms.txt` with only live canonical URLs.
- Consistent NAP, phone, service area, and business description.
- Short quotable definitions for acoustic treatment, soundproofing, RT60, NRC, PET panels, fabric panels, and ceiling baffles.

## Success Criteria

- No mixed host signals remain.
- Every indexable page has a canonical URL.
- Every money page has a specific title and meta description.
- The sitemap only lists live, canonical, indexable URLs.
- At least 20 commercial/use-case pages are mapped to search intent.
- Blog/resource articles internally link into service and product pages.
- Organic lead tracking is visible by page, source, and CTA type.
