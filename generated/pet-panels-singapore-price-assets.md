→ Wall installed: S$18–S$28 psf

→ Custom CNC: up to S$50 psf

The real cost drivers? Thickness, pattern, mounting method, and project size. A 40 sq ft home feature wall and a 400 sq ft office fit-out will be quoted very differently — and that's normal.

Rule we stick to: the cheapest panel isn't the one with the lowest psf. It's the one that solves the problem with the least material.
