## Instagram Caption

Your office echoes because glass, concrete, and bare walls reflect sound — every word bounces several times before it fades. The fix isn't soundproofing. It's absorption: cover 20–30% of your hard surfaces with acoustic panels, placed where sound hits first (the wall behind the screen, the ceiling above the meeting table). Most Singapore meeting rooms can be treated in a single day. Send us photos of your space and we'll map out exactly what you need.
