## Instagram Caption

PET panels vs fabric panels — the honest version.

PET: cheaper, tougher, humidity-proof, modern felt look. Perfect for offices, tuition centres, gyms and HDB rooms.

Fabric: premium finish, stronger bass absorption, endless colours. Right for boardrooms, fine dining, churches and studios.

The real answer for most Singapore projects? Mix them. PET on ceilings for coverage, fabric on a feature wall for finish. Better acoustics, better look, same budget.

Neither of them is soundproofing though — both reduce echo inside the room, they don't block noise between rooms.
