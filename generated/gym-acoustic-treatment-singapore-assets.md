Your gym doesn't need louder speakers. It needs less echo.

Most Singapore studios are built inside concrete shoplots with bare ceilings and mirrored walls — every beat, every cue, every dropped weight bounces back into the room. Coaches lose their voice. Members flinch at the chorus. Music sounds muddy above 80% volume.

Acoustic treatment fixes that. Panels on the ceiling and rear wall absorb the reflections so the music stays tight and coaches stop shouting.

It's not soundproofing — that's a different problem. This is about how the room sounds inside.
