## Instagram Caption

How much do acoustic panels cost in Singapore?

Quick answer: S$80–S$250 per m² installed.

• PET panels: S$80–140/m² (budget-friendly, great for speech)
• Fabric-wrapped: S$150–250/m² (premium, heavier absorption)
• Ceiling panels: S$200–350/m² (for high ceilings & open spaces)

For most offices and restaurants, a full room treatment lands between S$3K and S$15K. The biggest cost driver isn't the panel — it's placement.

A diagnosed S$6K job usually beats a blanket S$12K one.
