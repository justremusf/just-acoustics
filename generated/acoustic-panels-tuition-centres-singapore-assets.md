## Instagram Caption

Tuition centres in Singapore almost never have a loudness problem — they have an echo problem.

Concrete ceilings, glass partitions, tiled floors, 15 kids in a shophouse room = reverberation that smears consonants together. The teacher shouts. Students miss word endings. By Friday, the teacher is hoarse and the class is tuned out.

Acoustic panels fix the room, not the noise between rooms. Target ~25% coverage on ceiling + key walls, aim for RT60 around 0.5s, and your recorded Zoom lessons suddenly sound professional.

WhatsApp us a photo of your classroom for a quick recommendation.
