# Competitor Analysis: Singapore Acoustic Treatment

Analyzed: 2026-05-02  
Market: Acoustic panels, soundproofing, and acoustic treatment in Singapore.

## Recommendation

Compete on diagnosis, local project proof, room-specific outcomes, and ease of buying. Several competitors look strong on product range, certifications, sustainability, or architecture/specifier language. Just Acoustics can win by making the buying path clearer for business owners who want a room fixed, not a catalogue to study.

## Competitive Observations

### Product Depth

Aural-Aid, AID, Enocoustic, dB Acoustics, Cellyx, and Ljuds show broad product ecosystems: fabric panels, timber, PET, foam, wood wool, baffles, ceiling systems, acoustic testing, insulation, and wall lining.

**Implication:** Just Acoustics should avoid looking like a thin reseller. Service pages should show recommended systems by room type and explain why each product fits.

### Sustainability and Certifications

Competitors often mention eco-friendly materials, low VOC, fire ratings, local testing, Singapore Green Label, ASTM, ISO, and other standards.

**Implication:** Add trust copy for material safety, fire rating, installation practices, and supplier/manufacturer standards wherever accurate.

### Project Proof

Most competitors show some project/gallery proof. Just Acoustics already has projects and testimonials, but those assets should become SEO content, not only visual trust.

**Implication:** Each project should become a short case-study page with problem, room type, treatment used, photos, outcome, and internal links.

### Educational Content

Competitors explain sound absorption vs soundproofing, product choices, and acoustic concepts. This is a direct content battlefield.

**Implication:** Just Acoustics should publish practical decision content: pricing, panel quantity, what works for offices/restaurants/churches, and what acoustic panels cannot solve.
