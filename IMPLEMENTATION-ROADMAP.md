# SEO Implementation Roadmap

Analyzed: 2026-05-02  
Site: https://www.justacoustics.co

## Recommendation

Fix technical trust first, then expand commercial content. The site already has enough useful content to benefit from cleaner crawl signals and stronger internal linking.

## Phase 1: Foundation, Weeks 1-4

### Technical

1. Standardize canonical host on `https://www.justacoustics.co`.
2. Confirm `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, canonical URLs, schema URLs, OG URLs, and `llms.txt` all use the same host.
3. Add or verify canonical metadata on every indexable page.
4. Confirm `/pricing` is live and useful before keeping it in sitemap and `llms.txt`.
5. Keep `/shop` in the sitemap if the shop should be indexed.
6. Add baseline security headers.
7. Establish GA4, Google Search Console, conversion tracking, and call/WhatsApp click tracking.

### Content

1. Rewrite metadata for `/about`, `/contact`, `/products`, `/services`, `/blog`, key service pages, and key product pages.
2. Add stronger proof blocks to homepage, about, services, and contact.
3. Add clear pricing/cost-factor copy if exact prices are not published.

### Success Criteria

- Sitemap only lists live canonical URLs.
- No mixed host signals.
- Every indexable page has a canonical.
- All priority money pages have CTR-focused metadata.
- Lead tracking is testable.

## Phase 2: Expansion, Weeks 5-12

### Commercial Pages

Build or strengthen:

- `/services/acoustic-treatment-singapore`
- `/services/office-acoustic-treatment-singapore`
- `/services/restaurant-acoustic-panels-singapore`
- `/services/church-acoustic-treatment-singapore`
- `/services/tuition-centre-acoustic-panels-singapore`
- `/services/home-studio-acoustic-treatment-singapore`
- `/services/hdb-condo-echo-reduction`
- `/pricing` or `/acoustic-panels-price-singapore`

### Resource Content

Publish the first 12-week content calendar. Focus on pricing, treatment-vs-soundproofing, panel quantity, RT60, office echo, restaurant noise, church clarity, and HDB/condo echo.

### Internal Linking

- Homepage links to top use cases.
- Service pages link to product pages.
- Product pages link to applications and projects.
- Blog articles link to one commercial target each.
- Project pages link back to relevant service pages.

### Success Criteria

- 12 new or refreshed SEO assets live.
- Each money page has at least 5 internal links pointing to it.
- Service pages include FAQ and Service schema.
- Organic landing-page leads are visible in analytics.

## Phase 3: Scale, Weeks 13-24

### Project Proof

Turn project work into searchable case studies:

- Office meeting room.
- Restaurant/cafe/bar.
- Church/event space.
- Tuition centre/school.
- Gym/leisure.
- Studio/home theatre.
- Residential HDB/condo.

Each case study should include problem, room context, treatment choice, visual result, outcome, testimonial if available, and related service links.

### Authority

- Request detailed Google reviews after projects.
- Build directory and citation consistency.
- Pitch local interior/design, F&B, church, education, and facility-management resource lists.
- Add expert quote snippets from the team to key guides.

### Success Criteria

- 8-12 case studies live.
- Improved rankings for use-case terms.
- Review count and quality improve.
- More long-tail commercial impressions in GSC.

## Phase 4: Authority, Months 7-12

### Thought Leadership

Publish higher-authority assets:

- Singapore acoustic treatment cost guide.
- Acoustic design guide for interior designers.
- Restaurant noise benchmark guide.
- Office meeting-room acoustic checklist.
- Church speech-clarity checklist.
- Acoustic material comparison guide.

### GEO and AI Search

- Maintain clean `llms.txt`.
- Add concise answer blocks to priority pages.
- Ensure LocalBusiness schema is complete.
- Keep NAP consistent across major profiles.
- Build citations from reputable Singapore lists and industry resources.

### Success Criteria

- Just Acoustics becomes citation-worthy for Singapore acoustic treatment answers.
- Commercial pages rank for high-intent queries.
- Organic leads become a measurable acquisition channel.
- Content library supports sales conversations, not only traffic.

## Risks

- Publishing too many thin location/use-case pages will weaken quality.
- Treating soundproofing and acoustic treatment as interchangeable can damage trust.
- Product-only pages will underperform if they do not explain room outcomes.
- Rankings may improve before leads if conversion tracking and CTAs are weak.
