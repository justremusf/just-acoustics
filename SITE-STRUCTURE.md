# Recommended Site Structure

Analyzed: 2026-05-02  
Business type: Local service business, Singapore acoustic treatment and installation.

## Recommendation

Use a hub-and-spoke structure around room problems and service intent. The site should make it easy for Google and buyers to understand three things:

1. What Just Acoustics does.
2. Which rooms it serves.
3. Which products/treatments solve each room problem.

## Page Roles

### Homepage

Role: Primary brand and category entry point.  
Target: `acoustic panels Singapore`, `acoustic treatment Singapore`.  
Must include: proof, room applications, service process, reviews, FAQs, CTA.

### Services Hub

Role: Route buyers by room type and problem.  
Target: `acoustic treatment Singapore`, `echo control Singapore`, `acoustic panel installation Singapore`.  
Must include: application cards, problem descriptions, process, FAQs.

### Service Detail Pages

Role: Convert buyers with a specific room problem.  
Targets: office, restaurant, church, education, gym, studio, residential.  
Must include: acoustic problems, recommended treatments, project proof, FAQs, CTA.

### Products Hub

Role: Help buyers compare treatment types.  
Targets: wall panels, ceiling panels, custom print panels, PET panels, fabric panels.  
Must include: product comparison, use cases, installation notes, linked services.

### Pricing Page

Role: Capture cost-intent buyers and qualify leads.  
Target: `acoustic panels price Singapore`, `acoustic treatment cost Singapore`.  
Must include: cost factors, examples/ranges if possible, quote process, FAQs.

### Projects Hub

Role: E-E-A-T and conversion proof.  
Target: project and industry-specific long tail.  
Must include: filters by room type, client/category, solution, before/after proof.

### Blog / Resource Centre

Role: Capture problem-aware searches and feed internal links to money pages.  
Target: questions, comparisons, pricing, practical guides.  
Must include: strong internal linking, direct answers, clear CTA.

## Internal Linking Rules

- Every service page links to relevant product pages.
- Every product page links to relevant service/use-case pages.
- Every blog article links to one primary commercial page.
- Every project page links to one service page and one product page.
- Homepage links to top 6 commercial pages.
- Footer links should include core service, product, pricing, projects, blog, contact.

## Sitemap Quality Gates

Include only:

- Live `200` pages.
- Canonical `www` URLs.
- Indexable pages.
- Pages with unique value.

Exclude:

- `/studio`
- `/api`
- Thank-you pages.
- Utility/legal pages from high-priority SEO treatment, though they can remain accessible.
- Any thin autogenerated page without unique content.
